Shadow of Infinity

In the year 2157, humanity reached the pinnacle of space exploration, but the discovery of a mysterious artifact on a distant moon shook the foundations of science. The artifact, named the "Eye of Infinity," is a glowing sphere capable of distorting time and space, causing those who touch it to experience visions of possible futures and forgotten pasts.
